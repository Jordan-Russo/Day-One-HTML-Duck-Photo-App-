Duck Facts

Ducks Enjoy Eating:

  • Seeds & Grains (BUT AVOID Crackers AND BREAD)
  • Fruits (BUT AVOID CITRUS FRUITS AND AVOCADOS)
  • Vegetables(BUT AVOID SPINACH)
  • Fish & Insects

3 Things that all Ducks Need:

  1. Water to Bathe, Swim and Drink
  2. The Company of Friends to Socialize with
  3. Enough Space to Roam around and Exercise within
